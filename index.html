How it Works

Create multiple gifts

Upload PDFs or ebooks, or send links, promotion codes or other content in exchange for an email address.

Easily create opt-in forms

Create a form to capture email subscribers in exchange for your gift. You can create a pop up form, xsart bar, slider, or just copy HTML into your website or existing software.

We’ll send the gifts.

Every time someone opts in to your form, we’ll send them the correct gift.

We’ll pass along their email.

We’ll then pass their email to your email service provider, like Mailchimp or Aweber. You can choose the list and any details you want to record.
